Interactive Image Gallery

In my free time I've been working on restoring a 1974 Dodge Charger and in doing so I wanted to create a place where I could show people all the work I've done on the car. I also wanted a unique way to organize the images so I came up with an idea for an interactive image gallery. Click the picture on the left to see the image gallery in action! My initial idea was for an online repair manual where you could click sections of the car to get advice and repair diagrams relevant to that section of your car but I then morphed this idea into an image gallery to hold all of my car restoration pictures.
